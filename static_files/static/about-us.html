Why choose Gradient Boost?

Mentor guided learning

Mentors have the ability to make the process of learning a lot easier, more practical and easier to engage with. At Gradient Boost we assign you with data scientists/analyst mentor who will have regular calls with you to help you in areas where you are struggling, give you insights into how to better improve your skillsets, guide you through the curriculum and motivate you when you are feeling down.

Industry guided curriculum

In building our curriculum we consulted with multiple data scientists to better understand the industry and the most important skills they use on a day to day basis. This gives you access to a curriculum that is highly practical and forever adapting to what is currently needed.

Peer to Peer Learning

Sometimes the best person to help you is a peer who relates to your struggles and doubts. We created a community of highly engaged students who are willing to help each other grow.

Project based learning

Build projects to help you start building a portfolio to differentiate yourself from other candidates and to give you practical experience in implementing what you learn.

Learn at your own pace

Some people learn, some need more time to master a topic. Learn at a pace that is both appropriate for your learning style and schedule.

Affordability

Our course is priced to be affordable to all students with payment plans available to increase access.
