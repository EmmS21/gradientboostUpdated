Introduction to Data Science


Welcome to your online curriculum guide. You will have two tutorial sessions a week which will be used to ask any questions about material covered in a given week and present assignments and projects. As articulated in your introductory email you also have access to the community Slack, this can be used to post any questions about issues you may be encountering in your learning.

We advice that you make an attempt towards a solution when working on your assignments and projects. If you cannot find a working solution in 20 minutes, post your problem, attempted solution and rationale on Slack. Either the mentors, community manager or your peers will help guide you to a working solution. Like wise, feel free and empowered to help your peers with problems you may have already solved.

Remember: you may encounter difficult concepts but believe in yourself and know that we are here to help you learn. Do not give up.
